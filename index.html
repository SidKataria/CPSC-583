Dataset: Smoking prevalence % among adult (>15) males, worldwide

This revised data is a set of 142 countries and the smoking prevalence among male adults over a period of 16 years. This also includes the year each country legislated Tobacco Control Laws which restricts the commercialisation of tobacco and cigarettes as such. I made sure to also include a scale point of where and how much of these control laws have been legislated based on a point system which follows the descriptions of the WHO report on the global tobacco epidemic, 2017. The smoke free policies include indoor workspaces/public places, public transport, government facilities, hospitals, schools, child-care facilities, stadiums, restaurants, bars/pubs, casinos, hotels, etcâ€¦ With their scoring as follows: 0 - No Data, 1 - Complete absence of ban or up to two public places completely smoke free, 2 - Three to five public places completely smoke free, 3 - Six to seven public places completely smoke free, 4 - All public places under smoke free legislation, 5 - (4) but with Designated Smoke Rooms (DSRs). Similarly, for Health warnings on Packages, 0 - No data, 1 - No/small warnings, 2 - Medium size warnings missing some appropriate characteristic, 3 - Medium size warnings with all appropriate characteristics, 4 - Large warnings with all appropriate characteristics. And for Advertising bad: 0 - No data, 1 - Complete absence of ban or ban that does not cover national television or radio or print media, 2 - Ban on national TV & radio & print media only, 3 - Ban on national TV & radio & print media as well as on some but no all other forms of direct and/or indirect advertising, 4 - Ban on all forms of direct and indirect advertising. All information can also be found on https://www.who.int/tobacco/surveillance/policy/country_profile/en/

Data Visualization:

Original Visualization: A graph showcasing the countries (in the row, ordered alphabetically) with their smoking prevalence among adult males as bars represented in percentage values. Using the interaction feature, hovering over values, details of year of legislation, advertisement ban score, etc.. With the help of the scroll below the visualization, data of following countries can be viewed. The 'measure value' tool allows for individual selection of years. Thus, comparison between countries within a specific year can be drawn.
